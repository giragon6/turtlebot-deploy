Sea Turtle Nest Presence Prediction with Turtlebot

What is Turtlebot?

Turtlebot is a binary classification machine learning model based on easy-to-acquire data accompanied by a physical prototype bot that can collect that data to demonstrate feasability.

A Turtlebot Demo

Be Turtlebot in this simple demo! Send our model details about your location and characteristics of the beach around you to get a prediction of nest presence with a confidence value.

Not sure what values to enter? Generate some random data based on the mean and standard deviation of our model's dataset to get started!
